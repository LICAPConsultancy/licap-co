Our Role

What We Do

LICAP Consultancy FZCO is a UAE-based boutique advisory firm specialising in energy transition capital raises and deal origination. We act as Lead Arranger — the principal coordinating party between technology companies and institutional investors.

We manage the full capital raise lifecycle: from structuring the transaction and preparing investor materials, to coordinating outreach, managing the information process and supporting through to close.

Our lean, principal-led model allows us to move with the speed and focus that early and growth stage companies require — without the institutional overhead of a larger advisory house.

Lead Arrangement

We act as mandated Lead Arranger — structuring the raise, coordinating investor introductions and managing the full information process from NDA to close.

Deal Origination

We identify and develop deal opportunities across energy transition and alternative asset sectors, connecting technology companies with aligned institutional and strategic capital.

Investor Relations

We manage the investor process — materials preparation, outreach sequencing, NDA management, due diligence coordination and close.
